About Me

Hello! I'm Anurag Mahapatra, a first-year Bachelor of Technology student majoring in Computer Science and Engineering at VSSUT, Burla. Passionate about software development and technology, I'm eager to expand my skills and make meaningful contributions to the tech industry. With proficiency in Java, C, C++, and Python, along with a solid grasp of Object-Oriented Programming principles, I'm ready to tackle diverse challenges. Experienced in version control using Git and GitHub, I navigate Linux systems with ease and possess a strong foundation in computer systems architecture. Additionally, I have some experience in front-end development, as showcased by this website. Currently, I'm immersing myself in Data Structures and Algorithms to hone my problem-solving skills. I'm particularly excited about exploring backend development with Java and Spring Boot, and delving deeper into Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. Committed to clean coding practices and proficient in refactoring techniques, I prioritize building maintainable and scalable codebases. Always eager to learn and grow, I actively seek opportunities to collaborate on innovative projects and contribute to the tech community.
